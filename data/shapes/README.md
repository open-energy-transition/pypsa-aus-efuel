## Australian postcode boundaries (POA)

Source:
https://www.abs.gov.au/statistics/standards/australian-statistical-geography-standard-asgs-edition-3/latest-release

Dataset:
Postal Areas (POA) 2021 – Shapefile (GDA2020)

File:
POA_2021_AUST_GDA2020_SHP/

License:
© Commonwealth of Australia (Australian Bureau of Statistics). Data is provided under ABS terms of use.

Recommended citation:
Australian Bureau of Statistics (2021), Australian Statistical Geography Standard (ASGS) Edition 3 – Postal Areas (POA).

Notes:
- This dataset provides polygon boundaries for Australian postcodes (POA).
- The shapefile consists of multiple files (.shp, .dbf, .shx, .prj, etc.) which must be kept together in the same directory.
- The attribute `POA_CODE21` is used as the postcode identifier.
- Centroids of postcode polygons are computed and used to spatially map rooftop solar capacity (from the CER dataset) to PyPSA network nodes.
